Performance Alarm*

To view performance alarms:

  • Go to Performance Alarm to view all triggered performance alarms.
  • Click Refresh to get the most updated alarms.

To export performance alarms:

  1. Go to Performance Alarm.
  2. Click the arrow next to the Export button.
  3. You can choose to export your alarms in either HTML or CSV.

Note:

  • Support for Performance Alarms varies between Synology NAS models. For more information, please refer to www.synology.com.

____
*Available on specific models only.
