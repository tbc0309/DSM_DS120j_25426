Hardware & Power

Options including power recovery, Wake on LAN, beep control, fan speed and Memory Compression are readily available at Control Panel > Hardware & Power. You can also set the system to start up or shut down automatically at a specified time, enable hard drive hibernation, or connect a backup power device.

Note:

  • Wake on LAN and Memory Compression are available on specific models only. For more information, please visit Synology website.
  • Options of beep control and fan speed are not available on fan-less models, such as EDS14.
