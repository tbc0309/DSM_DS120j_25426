Account Protection

The Account Protection tab provides options related to Account Protection, such as canceling of the protection and managing trusted clients. To view this tab, click the person-shaped icon at the upper-right of the DSM desktop and select Personal.

Cancel protection

If your account becomes protected when a malicious attack occurs, you can cancel the protection yourself.

Trust current client

Once a client successfully logged in, it will be always trusted. If current client is untrusted, you can still reset it as trusted.

Manage trusted clients

All trusted clients of your account are listed.

Untrust selected clients

Select the clients which you want to untrust, and click Untrust.
