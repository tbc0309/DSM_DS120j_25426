Quota

Under Quota, you can view your quota on all the Synology NAS volumes set by admin, as well as the amount of capacity you have used on each volume. On models with Btrfs support, you can also view the quota and capacity usage of each shared folder.

To view this tab, click the person-shaped icon at the upper-right of the desktop and select Personal.

Note:

  • Btrfs is available on specific models only. To see if your model supports Btrfs, please refer to this article.
